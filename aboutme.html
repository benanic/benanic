About Me

Born in Croatia, I moved to the United States at a young age and have lived here ever since. I pursued my interest in finance and business analytics at the University of South Carolina, where I received my degree in Finance with a concentration in Business Analytics. Since graduating, I have gained two years of experience as a data analyst, primarily working with the Microsoft Power Platform stack. In this role, I have developed a strong set of skills and am always looking for ways to improve and stay up-to-date on the latest technologies in the field.

I am a hard-working and dedicated individual who takes pride in my work and am committed to delivering high-quality results. I am confident in my ability to analyze complex data sets and present clear, actionable insights to stakeholders. I am excited to continue my career in data analysis and am eager to take on new challenges and opportunities.

In my free time, I enjoy staying active and exploring new areas. I am also a big fan of traveling and experiencing different cultures. Overall, I am a driven and ambitious individual who is dedicated to achieving my professional goals and making a positive impact in my field.
